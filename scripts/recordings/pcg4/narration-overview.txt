Welcome to PCG4 — the Product Configurator Generation Four. This is where insurance products are designed, reviewed, and deployed. On the Guide page, you can see six key capabilities: Configuration Designer for building products visually, Approval Workflows for quality control, Version Control for tracking changes, Template Library for quick starts, Role-Based Access for granular permissions, and the Deployment Pipeline for publishing to production. The Configurations page shows all product configurations with their status — draft, in review, or published. Each configuration goes through the eight-step wizard: insurer details, product information, plan definitions, base configuration, encounter and benefit mapping, network tiers, overrides, and final review. The Reference Library provides curated data — ICD codes, procedure codes, and standard benefit templates. And the Coverage Mapper is where encounter types connect to benefit definitions — forty-four encounter categories covering preventive care, primary care, specialist consultations, emergency services, and inpatient hospitalization.
